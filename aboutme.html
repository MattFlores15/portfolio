WHO AM I?

Hi, I'm Matias. Currently based in the Netherlands, I am on an exciting journey to uncover my true passion as a UX Designer and Front-End Developer.

Originally from Chile, I've had the privilege of calling Argentina and Spain my homes before settling in the Netherlands. This diverse background has shaped me into a truly multicultural individual, fostering an open-minded and resourceful approach to design. Living across borders has given me a unique perspective on the fluidity of ideas and the beauty that emerges when cultures converge.

Above all, my family is my anchor. Raised by my incredible mother and big sister, they have been my mentors, guiding me through life's twists and turns. Their wisdom has not only influenced my personal values but has also significantly impacted my design philosophy. Their lessons on resilience and compassion reflect in my work, infusing it with a human-centric touch.
